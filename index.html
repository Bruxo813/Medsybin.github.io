What is Psilocybin?

Psilocybin is a naturally occurring psychedelic compound found in certain species of mushrooms, including Psilocybe cubensis, the only type of psilocybin mushroom allowed to be manufactured in Oregon. Psilocybe cubensis is known for its potent psilocybin content and is carefully cultivated for its therapeutic benefits.
